About Us

We Make People Happy

EuropeCruises.com is a division of WMPH Vacations, a Florida-based travel company specializing in cruises and resort vacations. WMPH Vacations acquired the EuropeCruises.com website address and brand in March of 2008 to complement our existing portfolios of websites including AlaskaCruises.com, EuropeCruises.com, HawaiiCruiseOutlet.com, and EuropeCruises.com. We launched the new EuropeCruises.com website on July 1, 2008.

Our travel partners include all major cruise lines including Carnival, Celebrity, Cunard, Disney, Holland America, Princess, Regent Seven Seas, and Royal Caribbean; and our cruise specialists are trained in all cruise destinations including Alaska, the Bahamas, Bermuda, the Caribbean, Hawaii, Europe and the Panama Canal.

WMPH is short for "We Make People Happy :-)." While our name may be cute and bring a grin to your face, it's a philosophy we take very seriously. Making you happy is our purpose. In fact, we are so serious about this, we named our company after it.

Our Mission

  • Value and Savings

Like most Top Producing travel agencies, we are among the first notified with last minute deals, senior discounts, regional promotions and group offers. We also use the latest computer reservations systems to enable our agents and our websites to help you find the best deals both online and off.

  • Service & Expert Advice

    EuropeCruises.com Cruise Specialists are knowledgeable, friendly and enthusiastic experts who know the cruise industry. Our cruise specialists are well trained and have the resources and tools they need to offer you a high level of service at low prices.

Our expertise comes from years of personal travel experience, as well as the travel experience of thousands of clients we have served in our careers.

  • Information

    Information is the number one reason why people go to the Internet. By providing honest, accurate information that is relevant to your cruise vacation and combining it with great prices and great service, we will exceed your expectations in helping you plan your cruise vacation.

Our website is conveniently available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week and is full of information that will help with your reservation. WMPH Vacations

WMPH Vacations was founded by Uf Tukel and Don Walker, former executives of Cruise Outlet of the Carolinas, Alamo Rental Car, MyTravel USA and CruisesOnly.

CLIA Hall of Fame Members -- Outstanding Cruise Retailers

Uf Tukel and Don Walker were chosen as inductees into the 2014 Cruise Lines International Association (CLIA) Hall of Fame, formally recognized as "Outstanding Cruise Retailers" for distinguishing themselves as a leader in cruise vacation sales and travel distribution.

The CLIA Hall of Fame honors those individuals, past and present, whose efforts and accomplishments represent a significant contribution to, and a positive impact on, the cruise industry. Honorees are selected based on open nominations from CLIA's membership, followed by a review by the CLIA Trade Relations Committee.

WMPH is a member of the Cruise Line International Association (CLIA); and, we are fully licensed, insured and bonded in the State of Florida. Florida Seller of Travel Registration Number is ST35780.

WMPH Vacations is proud to be a member of the Better Business Bureau.
